An alternative approach to road racing

The bike

Built by Tom Parkes
Hand built with no influence from exisiting designs

With a unique suspension design

A rising rate, twin wishbone construction offering remarkable control under braking and plush suspension movement

Raced at the toughest race track in the world

Where better to develop a suspension design than in between stone walls on public roads?
